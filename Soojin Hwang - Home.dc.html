01

T-Mobile Agentic AI

Led 0→1 design of T-Mobile's first AI assistant, turning the overwhelming switching journey into a guided conversation. Three moments from the shipped product, across web, iOS, and Android.

Lead Product Designer · 2024–2026 View case study →
9:41

Agentic bill assistant. The entry moment: the assistant reads the bill, explains what changed, and stays ready for follow-ups. One tap, one clear answer.

Voice-led multimodality. As the conversation moves, the interface follows. Options and visuals surface only when they help. People talk to it, they do not manage it.

Always within reach. The assistant stays present on every screen. While browsing devices, people can compare options, surface the promotions they qualify for, or start an upgrade — without leaving the flow they're in.

02

T-Mobile Coverage Map

Redesigned how the millions who check T-Mobile's coverage each year read network data, turning dense signal maps into something anyone understands at a glance.

Lead Product Designer · Web · 6.2M annual visitors View case study →

Will this work where I live. Search an address and see your exact block. The map answers the one question people actually have.

Dense data, read at a glance. National signal data redesigned to be legible in seconds, for the 6.2M people who check it every year.

Where the app already lives. Coverage sits alongside the everyday map people use to find family and places—one tap from where they already are.

A verdict, not a legend. A searched address returns one plain answer—strong outdoors, limited indoors—instead of a color key to decode.
